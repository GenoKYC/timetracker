What is TimeTracker?

TimeTracker is a modern solution for recording Time In, Time Out, and Leave — helping individuals and teams manage their work hours with ease and accuracy.

Whether you're a freelancer, team leader, or HR manager, TimeTracker provides a clear and reliable record of attendance and leave history, making it easier to manage work hours and ensure transparency.

Our Key Features

Easy Time in & Out

Clock in and out with just one click — no more spreadsheets or manual logs.

Leave Management

Request and approve leaves directly in the platform. Stay organized and hassle-free.

Attendance Dashboard

See your daily logs, leave balances, and total hours in one clean view.
